Garlic butter pasta

A quick and flavorful garlic butter pasta made with simple ingredients like spaghetti, butter, garlic, and Parmesan. Perfect for a fast and delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss well. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
